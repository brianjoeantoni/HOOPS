FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



Q. How do purchase my order on the site?


  1. Click "Add to cart" button on the products you wish to purchase.
  2. Click "Continue Shopping" to continue add other products into the cart or click "Checkout" to proceed your payment for the products.
  3. You'll reach a log in page where you need to log in your account using your Username and Password. If you don't have an account created yet, you can checkout as a guest or you can register an account before you proceed your payment.
  4. You may select to deliver your item or pick-up at our selected stores.
  5. If you select delivery, you'll need to enter your shipping address or select your previously saved shipping address.
  6. If you select pick-up you'll need select our stores that allow pick-up and your pick-up time.
  7. Next you'll need to choose your payment method then click "next" button.
  8. You may also enter e-voucher at this step if you have one.
  9. After the steps before this have been done, You need to reconfirm your billing details and agree on the terms and condition.
  10. Click "Process Order" to complete the order.

Q. What are the payment methods available?


    We offers various kind of Payment Methods as shown below :


  1. E-Wallets (OVO / DANA / Go-Pay)
  2. Internet Banking / Online Bank Transfer
  3. Transfer Indomaret / Alfamart

Q. What is the product's transaction currency?


All products transaction currency will be done in Indonesian Rupiah.


Q. How long does the delivery usually takes?


Delivery usually will takes about 1 to 3 working days depending on the courrier company service level.


Q. Are the products counterfeit?


No, our products are 100% original from their own respective brand.
